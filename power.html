Power and Corruption

Characters like Silco and Mel exemplify the corrupting influence of power. Silco's relentless pursuit of Zaun's independence leads him down a path of moral compromise, as he manipulates and controls to achieve his vision. Similarly, Mel's political maneuvering in Piltover showcases how the desire for control can erode ethical boundaries. Their journeys highlight the complexities and consequences of wielding power in a morally ambiguous world.
