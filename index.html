About Arcane

Genre: Action, Drama, Sci-Fi, Fantasy

Based on: The League of Legends universe

Streaming on: Netflix


Synopsis

Arcane is a visually stunning animated series that explores the origin stories of two iconic League of Legends champions: Vi and Jinx. Set in the dual cities of Piltover, a gleaming hub of innovation, and Zaun, its polluted, oppressed underbelly, the show dives into themes of class divide, power, and the cost of progress.


As tensions rise between the two cities, the invention of a powerful new magical technology, Hextech, threatens to upset the fragile balance. At the same time, personal conflicts erupt among the cast of characters. Sisters are torn apart, alliances shift, and the line between good and evil blurs.
