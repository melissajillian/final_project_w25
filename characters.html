Meet the Characters

Jinx

Unpredictable, ingenious, and chaotic. She embodies the series' exploration of trauma and madness.

Vi

A tough, street-smart woman turned enforcer with powerful gauntlets and a soft spot for her sister.

Vander

Once the protector of Zaun, who is a father figure whose past shapes the city's future.

Silco

A Zaunite criminal and revolutionary driven by ambition and trauma. He seeks to free Zaun, no matter the cost.

Jayce

Visionary inventor from Piltover. Ambitious, powerful, and deeply conflicted.

Viktor

A brilliant scientist chasing transcendence, torn between humanity and progress.

Ekko

A brilliant Zaunite inventor who leads the Firelights and has a fierce love for Zaun's future.

Heimerdinger

A wise yordle and revered inventor, cautious of the dangers hidden in rapid innovation.

Mel

An intelligent, powerful, and ambitious heiress, who is a Piltover councilor and a mage.

Caitlyn

Chose to become an Enforcer to pursue true justice. She is known for her intelligence and resourcefulness.

Sevika

Silco's ruthless right hand, who is a realize and armed with a mechanical arm as well as unshakeable loyalty.

Isha

A non-verbal Zaunite orphan taken in by Jinx, who becomes her sister figure and guardian.

Ambessa

Mel's formidable mother, who is a Noxian warlord with power in every word and strike.

Maddie

An Enforcer in Caitlyn's elite team, who represents the new generation of Piltover law enforcement.

Singed

A twisted alchemist whose experiments push the boundaries of science and morality.
